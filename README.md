# 🩸 GlucoScope

> **Understand today. Improve tomorrow.**

A modern, open-source Nightscout dashboard designed to help people living with diabetes better understand their glucose data, discover daily patterns, and make informed decisions every day.

GlucoScope is more than a dashboard.

It aims to become an AI-powered diabetes companion for daily management, personal insights, healthcare discussions, and content creation.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Live Dashboard

- Real-time glucose monitoring
- 24-hour glucose chart
- Time in Range (TIR)
- Time Above Range (TAR)
- Time Below Range (TBR)
- Average Glucose
- Coefficient of Variation (CV)
- Glucose Management Indicator (GMI)
- AI Daily Score *(Planned)*

---

### 🤖 AI Insights *(Planned)*

- Daily glucose analysis
- Personalized coaching
- Trend comparison
- Pattern detection
- Weekly and monthly summaries

---

### ✍ Creator Tools *(Planned)*

Generate content with a single click.

- note article
- Instagram post
- Threads post
- X (Twitter) post
- Daily glucose summary image

---

### 🏥 Medical Support *(Planned)*

- Clinic-friendly reports
- PDF export
- 90-day statistics
- HbA1c trend estimation
- TIR history

---

## 🌍 Data Sources

GlucoScope works with any glucose data available through the Nightscout API.

Current primary environment:

- ✅ MiniMed 780G
- ✅ Guardian Monitor
- ✅ Nightscout

Future compatibility:

- Libre 2 / Libre 3 (via Nightscout)
- Dexcom G7 (via Nightscout)
- Other Nightscout-compatible CGM systems

## 🎯 Philosophy

Diabetes management isn't only about numbers.

It's about understanding what happened today,
learning from daily experiences,
and making tomorrow just a little bit better.

That's why GlucoScope focuses on simplicity,
clarity,
and meaningful insights instead of overwhelming charts.
